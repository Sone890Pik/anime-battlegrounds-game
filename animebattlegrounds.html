How to Play

  1. Select a Character: Click on the character card of your choice (Naruto, Goku, or Luffy). Their stats will be displayed.
  2. Train Your Stats: Use the "Train HP," "Train Attack," and "Train Defense" buttons to increase your character's stats. Each training session will slightly improve the stat.
  3. Battle: Once you're ready, click the "Start Battle" button. You will face a randomly chosen opponent.
  4. Battle Log: The battle log will display the sequence of events, showing how much damage each character deals.
  5. победитель: The first character to reduce their opponent's HP to 0 wins the battle. A winning message will be displayed.
  6. Reset: After a battle, you can click the "Reset" button to start over, choose a new character, and train again.

Train your character wisely to become the strongest!
